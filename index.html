About Us

IIIT Lucknow

IIIT Lucknow established its incubation “Centre for innovation, incubation and Entrepreneurship, IIIT Lucknow” in 2021 to promote research and incubation activities, seminars, conferences, workshops for start-up companies/entrepreneurs and many more for effective entrepreneurship.

The cell is dedicated to promote growing entrepreneurs and serve as an effective pillar towards building up and supporting avant-garde start-ups.

In doing so, IIIT Lucknow signed an MOU with SIIC, IIT Kanpur to facilitate collaboration and promote start-ups. We also collaborated with industry experts to offer mentorship to our start-ups.
